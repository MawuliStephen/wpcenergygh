Ghana’s Committee Structure

WPC Energy Ghana operates with a local-level committee structure, mirroring the framework of the WPC Energy Secretariat. These committees are designed to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of WPC Energy Ghana’s operations, ensuring strong engagement with Ghana’s energy sector.

The Council

  • Governing body of WPC Energy Ghana. Convenes once a year
  • Made up of about 60 national committees from member countries.
  • Elects the President and an Executive Committee (EC) to develop and execute its strategy
  • Selects the host country for the next WPC Energy Congress

Executive Committee (EC)

  • National Committees can nominate candidates to the EC. The Council selects the EC members as stated in the constitution

Congress Programme Committee (CPC)

  • National Committees can nominate senior experts from their countries and the Council selects the best candidates. WPC Energy Vice President for Programme and Chair of the CPC may appoint additional members to the CPC to provide a good balance of expertise, gender and geography
  • Main role: to oversee the development and planning of the Technical Programme and support other content areas of the Congress Programme

Young Professionals Committee (YPC)

  • Set up in 2006 to give a voice to young professionals in the energy sector
  • Each National Committee is invited to include two young professionals who focus on the various YP Projects as well as give their input on content for the Congress YP Programme and have representative on the CPC via the Chair of the YPC
