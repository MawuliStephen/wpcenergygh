About Us

Energy is essential to sustain life and power our world. Recognizing this, nature has provided us with various fuel sources that drive modern society. However, with this gift comes a responsibility: to utilize these energy resources equitably while safeguarding the future of our planet.

WPC Energy, the global forum for energy transformation, is dedicated to promoting the sustainable management and use of the world's energy resources for the benefit of all. To accomplish this, WPC Energy focuses on these key pillars:

  • Non Advocacy, non-political.
  • Facilitating dialogue among stakeholders across the energy to address global energy issues.
  • Promoting a realistic understanding of what is required for an effective energy transformation.
  • Empowering Young Professionals for the future of the sector - Youth Forum.

WPC Energy Ghana

WPC Energy Ghana is a distinguished member of WPC Energy, having been officially admitted to the council on January 12, 2021. Ghana’s membership was granted after fulfilling all necessary requirements, with the primary criterion being its status as an oil-producing nation.


Ghana's membership in WPC Energy is designed to ensure that the organization’s mission and vision have a direct and meaningful impact on the Ghanaian people.


WPC Energy Ghana is deeply committed to the well-being and progress of Ghana, guided by its mission:

Empowering tomorrow, today: Innovating energy, advancing sustainable oil and gas practices, and collaborating for a cleaner, brighter world.


Our vision is to empower a sustainable future by pioneering innovative energy solutions, promoting responsible oil and gas practices, and fostering global collaboration for a cleaner, brighter world.

WPC Energy World

WPC Energy is a non-advocacy, non-political organisation with charitable status in the UK and accreditation as a Non-Governmental Organisation from the United Nations. Formed in 1933 and comprising 60 National Committees, WPC Energy facilitates an open dialogue around energy and its products and is dedicated to the promotion of their sustainable management for the benefit of all.

Through this mission and vision, WPC Energy Ghana aims to:

  • Drive sustainable energy development that benefits all Ghanaians
  • Promote environmentally responsible practices in the oil and gas sector.
  • Promote environmentally responsible practices in the oil and gas sector.
  • Empowering Young Professionals for the future of the sector - Youth Forum.

By aligning with WPC Energy’s global efforts, WPC Energy Ghana is dedicated to creating a future where energy resources are used equitably and sustainably, ensuring prosperity for current and future generations.
